A Blend of Old and New

19 March 2025

When I look around the Arab world in 2025, I see something I’ve never quite seen before, a perfect blend of tradition and transformation. Walking through the streets of Dubai or catching a coffee in Amman, I feel the pulse of a region that’s not just keeping up with the world, but innovating in its own way. It’s not surprising anymore to meet young Arabs who code, design, build apps, or start businesses. It’s like we’re finally writing our own future. One that still respects our roots but isn’t afraid to evolve.

More Than Just Hope

27 March 2025

Sure, things aren't perfect. We still face tough questions about politics, jobs, the environment but there’s this quiet strength in people now. It feels like we’re done waiting for change. We’re building it. From local communities to digital spaces, I see Arabs taking charge, pushing forward, and lifting each other up. 2025 isn’t just a year, it’s a reminder that we’ve made it through so much, and we’re just getting started.
